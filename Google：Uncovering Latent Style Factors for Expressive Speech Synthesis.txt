语音效果： at https://google.github.io/tacotron/publications/uncovering_ latent_style_factors_for_expressive_speech_synthesis/

笔记：
    主要是在Tacotron中引入"style tokens"的概念，建立韵律模型，以纯数据驱动的方式自动学习各种韵律变化。最终，我们可以用某种可预测的、全局一致的方式控制
合成语音的韵律风格。
    主要是在Tacotron中引入style encoder，平行的计算text attention和style token attention，并且通过权重加和，把他们结合起来。style tokens可以解释
为隐变量，编码那些单独以文本作为输入无法捕捉的变化。text encoder是以text sequence作为输入，与text encoder不同，style tokens没有输入，style tokens
的embedding values都是随机初始化的并且通过后向传播自动学习得到。And the tokens are shared across all training sequences.

模型训练结束以后：
    After training, we can “listen” to each style token by forcing the style attention to attend to the speciﬁed token. Since the training
process learns to rely on a mixture of tokens, this method can lead to unintelligible speech, but it nonetheless provides a rough idea of
the prosodic style each token corresponds to. 
    To synthesize speech in a speciﬁc style, we can broadcast-add the embedding vector of the selected style token to the full style
embedding matrix, thereby biasing synthesis towards the speciﬁed style. Similarly, we can mix different styles by consecutively broadcast-
adding or linearly interpolating style embedding vectors. Other mixing methods can achieve similar effects, and we defer a study of them
to future work.
